Vegetable Lasagna

Ingredients:

14 lasagna noodles

2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

1 cup chopped onion

3 crushed garlic cloves

1/8 tsp red pepper flakes

2 medium zucchini, chopped

2 medium yellow squash, chopped

425g roasted red peppers

800g crushed tomatoes

chopped fresh basil leaves

425g ricotta cheese

2 large eggs

60g parmesan cheese

230g mozzarella cheese

salt and pepper

Method:

Cook Noodles:

- Add salt to some water, and bring to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles.

Prepare Vegetables:

- Heat the oven to 180 C. Line a baking pan with parchment.

- Heat olive oil in a wide skillet. Add the onions, cook till translucent. Add garlic, red pepper flakes, zucchini, squash and a pinch of salt. Cook for about 7 minutes.

- Stir in the roasted red peppers and crushed tomatoes. Bring to a low simmer and cook for about 7 minutes. Add the basil, salt and pepper.

Cheese Filling:

- Combine ricotta, eggs, and 1/2 tsp of salt until smooth.

To End:

- Spoon enough vegetable mixture into the baking dish.
