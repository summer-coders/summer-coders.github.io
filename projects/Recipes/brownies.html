Brownies Recipe

Ingredients:

145g unsalted butter

250g granulated sugar

80g unsweetened cocoa powder

1/4tsp salt

1tsp vanilla extract

2 large eggs

65g all-purpose flour

Method:

- Heat the oven to 160 C. Line the baking paper with parchment or aluminium foil.

- Make a bain-marie by pouring a bit of water in a saucepan. Put the butter, sugar, cocoa powder and salt in a bowl, and rest it on the saucepan such that the bowl does not touch the water. Stir occasionally to avoid burning and till the butter is melted.

- Remove from the heat the set it aside for a while to cool till warm.

- Mix in vanilla. Add the eggs one at a time, combining well after each one.

- Add the flour and mix well. The batter will be thick. Pour it into the baking pan and bake for 20-25 mins. Cool completely, then cut and serve.
