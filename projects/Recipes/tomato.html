Tomato Basil Pizza with Sea Salt

Ingredients:

340g pizza dough

2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil

1 tsp flaked sea saltr

1 1/2 cups cheese

basil leaves

2 large eggs

8 cherry tomatoes, halved

Method:

- Heat the oven to 200 C. Line the baking sheet with parchment paper.

- Form the pizza dough to the size of the pan. Coat it with the olive oil and sprinkle the sea salt around the edges. Bake for 8 minutess.

- Spread the cheese, tomatoes and basil leaves over the crust, but not on the salt. Drizzle more olive oil on the pizza.

- Bake for another 8 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and edges lightly browned.

- Cut and serve.
