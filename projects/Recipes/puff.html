Puff Pastry Pinwheels

Ingredients:

puff pastry sheets

1 large egg

1 tbsp water

1/2 cup chopped roasted bell peppers

1/2 cup cheese spread

30g cheese

2 tbsp chopped parsley or basil

Method:

- Heat the oven to 200 C. Line the baking paper with parchment or aluminium foil.

- Beat the egg and water in a bowl.

- Lightly roll the puff pastry sheet out, just to flatten any creases.

- Brush the sheet with the egg-water mixture. Spread the cheese spread then sprinkle the peppers, cheese and herbs. Add any anything else, if required.

- Roll the pastry like a log cake, and cut into 1/2 inch pieces. Place the slices onto a baking sheet and brush the tops with egg wash.

- Bake until light brown, about 15 minutes. Serve warm.
